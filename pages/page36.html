Their bodies and sizes were very different: some were short, others were long and clumsy. They had no fur. They had just a bit of hair on their body, and that was all. moreover, they did not know how to fight. They ran up to White Fang and tried to attack him with their short teeth. White Fang fought with them gladly. They were not dangerous enemies to him at all. Later on, it became a game. White Fang ran after the dogs and started to fight for his own amusement. Thus White Fang's days were enjoyable and easy. But he was still very lonely and sad. He was the enemy of every dog he had met on his way.
