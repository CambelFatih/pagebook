Chapter 9

The crazy God

There were not many white men who lived in fort Yukon. The men in the fort did not like the newcomers. But they liked the fights among the new comers' dogs and White Fang very much. When a ship arrived, the men of the fort came down to the bank and watched the fight. It was a tradition, so to say. They liked the way White Fang fought and showed his anger to the new dogs. But there was one man among them who really enjoyed the fight. He used to run to the bank at the first sound of the new ships, and when the fight was over he returned sadly to the fort. Sometimes, when White Fang killed a weak dog, this man would cry loudly with joy. Then he would look at White Fang enviously. The other men called this man "Beauty Smith." Nobody knew his real name. That is why they called him Beauty Smith. He was a very ugly man. nature had been greedy with him. He was not tall. His body was small and thin. His forehead was very wide and his eyes were large. His face was big. His teeth were large and yellow. Beauty Smith was weak and a coward. In short, Beauty Smith looked really ugly and disgusting. nature had made him that way. He did the cooking and dish-washing for the other men in the fort. The men in the fort did not like him. They were even afraid of him and his ugliness. This was the man who looked at White Fang so enviously. He wanted to have him. White Fang did not like Beauty Smith. He snarled at him angrily and showed him his sharp teeth. White Fang felt the evil in this man. He always tried to stay away from him. So he hated Beauty Smith from the very beginning.
