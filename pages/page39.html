At the fort, Beauty Smith tied White Fang and went to bed. White Fang waited for an hour. Then he began to howl loudly and bitterly. Beauty Smith woke up and hit him without mercy. Beauty Smith did it very gladly. He listened to White Fang's cries of pain with joy. White Fang knew that his master had given him to Beauty Smith. He did not love Grey Beaver, but he was still loyal to him. After the beating, Beauty Smith tied a chain around White Fang's neck and went to bed again. White Fang was very sad. He simply could not believe it at first. But it was the truth. White Fang felt a terrible pain all over his body. The next day he was sick. He could not move his body at all. After a few days, Grey Beaver left Yukon and started his long journey to the Mackenzie River. White Fang stayed in the Yukon with his crazy master. But what could White Fang do? To White Fang Beauty Smith was a crazy and terrible god. Anyway, he had to live with his new master and obey him at once.
