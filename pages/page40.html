Chapter 10

The reign of Hate

Under the control of the crazy god White Fang became a devil. Beauty Smith kept him chained in a pen in the backyard of the fort. There he hit White Fang and drove him wild every day. The man soon discovered White Fang's hatred of laughter and laughed at him loudly and scornfully, pointing his finger at him the whole time. At such times, White Fang was beside himself with anger. He looked even crazier than Beauty Smith. Presently, White Fang became the enemy of all living things men and dogs. He was more terrible than ever. He hated blindly and without reason. He hated everything around him: the chain that held him, the men who looked at him fearfully, the dogs that snarled at him angrily, and the pen that kept him in the same place every day. And, most of all, he hated Beauty Smith. Beauty Smith had a purpose in all that he did to White Fang. He knew very well how to earn his money with the help of White Fang. One day, many men gathered about the pen. Beauty Smith entered the pen and took off the chain from White Fang's neck. Then he went out and opened the door of the pen. White Fang was very surprised at that moment. Something unusual was happening. He waited. The door was still open. Then his master pushed a big dog inside and closed the door behind him. White Fang had never seen such a dog. It was a mastiff. But the size and dangerous look of the dog did not stop White Fang. He saw something he could release all of his anger and hatred on.
