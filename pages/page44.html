Chapter 11

The fight to the Death

Beauty Smith took the chain off of his neck and stepped back. This time White Fang did not make a quick attack. He had never seen such a dog before. He stood still and looked at his enemy attentively. The bull-dog went to the center of the circle and stopped stupidly. There were many cries from the crowd, "Get him, Cherokee! Kill this wolf!" But Cherokee did not move. He was not afraid of White Fang, he was just lazy. But then Cherokee began to snarl at White Fang softly and slowly. The hair on White Fang's neck began to rise with anger. Then he attacked suddenly. There was a loud cry of joy in the crowd. The bull-dog did not even snarl in return. He just stood still in the center of the circle. White Fang attacked him again and again. His enemy seemed to ignore all his blows and snarls. This action had some unknown purpose. White Fang could not understand why the dog ignored him. He was very surprised. The bull-dog had no hair for protection, and he did not try to protect himself at all. He did not run after White Fang, either. White Fang did his best to bite his enemy's throat, but he failed every time. Cherokee's neck was too short and his head was too massive. Time went by. White Fang still tried to bite his enemy's throat. And still the bull-dog did not attack him. There was a big difference in their sizes. Cherokee was very small and White Fang was big and quick. After a while, White Fang attacked his enemy's shoulder. At that moment the bull-dog turned to him quickly and closed his teeth around White Fang's throat. White Fang ran around trying to push his enemy away.
