But Cherokee did not release his teeth. White Fang was beside himself with anger and fear. He could do nothing in this position. It was like a trap. It was a crazy moment. The life that was in him cried loudly. He wanted to release his body as soon as possible. His intelligence seemed to disappear slowly. White Fang moved left and right, but all in vain. Cherokee knew exactly what he was doing. It was his fighting method, so to say. White Fang was tired and powerless. He could do nothing. He could not understand anything. This kind of attack had never happened to him in his life. Cherokee's teeth were closing in on his throat. It was like fate itself. The bull-dog was killing White Fang slowly. Beauty Smith was very angry. He laughed at White Fang and pointed his finger at him scornfully. But White Fang could do nothing to release himself. He was in a trap. The men cried loudly, "Cherokee! Cherokee!" Then a man went up to the pen. It was a dog-musher. Another man followed him quickly. At that moment White Fang stopped fighting. All his power left him at that time. His eyes began to close slowly. Beauty Smith ran up to White Fang and started to beat him with his boots without mercy. At that moment the dog-musher ran quickly into the fighting circle. Then he struck Beauty Smith with a heavy blow. Beauty Smith fell on the ground at once. The dog-musher looked very angry. He turned to the crowd and cried loudly, "You are wild animals!" After that his companion went up to him and bent over the dogs. They both tried to separate the fighting dogs on the ground. The dog-musher's name was Scott. "Matt, we have to pull the bull-dog's jaw open very carefully. otherwise, the wolf could die at any moment," said Scott to his companion.
