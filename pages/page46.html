After some time they opened the bull-dog's jaw successfully. Then they pushed Cherokee back to his master. Matt examined White Fang carefully. At that moment Beauty Smith came up to White Fang. Scott looked at Beauty Smith with disgust.srt "Mr Beast, I'm going to buy your dog. I'll give you one hundred and fifty dollars for him," said Scott. "I won't sell him," said Beauty Smith. "No, you'll sell your dog right now!" cried Scott. "Here is your money. The dog is mine. You have no right to have this dog. Understand?" Scott handed his money to Beauty Smith. Beauty Smith did not say a word. He took his money and went away quickly. Some of the men were going away, others were talking to each other quietly. "Who is that man?" asked Cherokee's master. "Weedon Scott," answered one of the men in the crowd. "He knows many people in the local government. If you don't want trouble, you'll leave with your dog right now."
