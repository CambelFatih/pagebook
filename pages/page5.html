Chapter 1

battle of the Fangs

A large pack of wolves ran quickly among the snowdrifts. At the front of the pack ran a big grey wolf. He was the leader of the pack and the strongest wolf among them. It was he who began to run faster and then looked at the she-wolf. He did not snarl at her when she tried to outrun him. On the contrary, he wanted to run near to her. But on her other side ran a strong, old wolf. His muzzle and body had the scar of many battles. He had only one eye. He always ran on her right side. At such times the old wolf did his best to touch her body, shoulder, or neck. But the she-wolf did not like this. She rejected his attention with her angry snarl and sharp teeth every time. The situation in the pack was desperate The lack of food and rising anger went together. The wolves were very weak and thin. But they ran as quickly as they could. At the back of the pack ran the old wolves, and at the front were the strongest ones. They ran many miles that day without stopping. They ran in a frozen and dead world. Nothing moved. They alone were alive, and they were looking for anything else that could survive the harsh conditions. Luckily they found a dead moose in the snow. They ate it at once. After that they slept for a long time with their full stomachs. The hunger was over. The next morning the pack ran down to the Mackenzie River. Each day one or two wolves died on the way. In the end, there were only three wolves left: the she-wolf, the one-eyed wolf, and the leader of the pack. The she-wolf was very angry at the time. Both the old wolf and young wolf had the marks of her teeth on them. They were kind to the she-wolf, but they were very angry at one another.

Her zaman onun sağ yanında koşardı. Bu zamanlarda yaşlı kurt, onun bedenine, omuzuna veya boynuna dokunmak için elinden geleni yapardı. Ama dişi kurt bundan hoşlanmıyordu. Her seferinde öfkelendi ve sivri dişleriyle onun ilgisini reddetti. Sürüdeki durum umutsuzdu. Yiyecek yokluğu ve artan öfke bir arada gidiyordu. Kurtlar çok zayıf ve inceydi. Ama en hızlı koşanları öndeydi. Arkada ise yaşlı kurtlar koşuyordu. O gün durmadan çok uzun mesafeler koştular. Donmuş ve ölü bir dünyada koştular. Hiçbir şey hareket etmiyordu. Sadece onlar hayattaydı ve zorlu koşullarda hayatta kalabilen başka herhangi bir şey arıyorlardı. Neyse ki, karın içinde ölü bir geyik buldular. Hemen yediler. Bundan sonra dolu karnıyla uzun süre uyudular. Açlık sona erdi. Ertesi sabah sürü, Mackenzie Nehri'ne indi. Yolda bir veya iki kurt her gün ölüyordu.
