Chapter 13

The Love-master

White Fang was watching Weedon Scott carefully. Twenty-four hours had passed since he had bitten his hand. And White Fang waited for his punishment to come. How could it be otherwise? He had bitten the hand of the god, and something terrible waited for him surely. Weedon Scott sat down a few steps away from him. White Fang could see nothing dangerous in that. Besides, the god had neither a club nor a gun in his hand. moreover, he was still free. No chain kept him anymore. So he decided to wait and see. The god did not move at all. Shortly, the god began to speak kindly and slowly. White Fang snarled at him for some time. But the god continued to talk to him, with a gentleness that touched White Fang's heart deeply. In spite of his instincts, White Fang began to trust this god. He felt safe next to this god. Suddenly, the god stood up and went into the house. White Fang looked at him carefully when he came out. He held a piece of meat in his hand. White Fang was very surprised. The punishment still had not happen. The next moment the god threw him the meat and said a kind word to him. White Fang smelled the meat and ate it quickly. Still nothing happened. Then the god threw another piece of meat from his hand. The same thing happened many times. The meat was very tasty and White Fang was hungry. But soon the god did not throw the meat as usual. He held it in his hand and looked at White Fang attentively. White Fang went up to the god and ate the meat from his hand. Still nothing happened. He still waited for his punishment, as it seemed. The god continued to talk to him softly.
