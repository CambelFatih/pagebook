White Fang. White Fang did not run to him like the other dogs. He just stood still near the stove. "God! Look at his tail! He's wagging his tail!" cried Matt. Weedon Scott went up to his dog quickly. Then he looked at him kindly. He sat down and began to stroke White Fang softly as usual. White Fang was snarling in return. But that was not all. At that moment White Fang found a new way to show his crazy love to his master. He suddenly pushed his head forward and put it between his master's arm and body. There he continued to growled softly. The two men looked at each other with surprise. White Fang came back to life at once. The return of his master was enough for him to live normally again. Life was moving in him quickly. His happiness had no limits at that moment. One night, Scott and Matt heard a loud noise. They rose to their feet and ran out of the house quickly. There was a wild scream in the air. Matt put the lamp in front of him and saw a man lying on his back in the snow. He was trying to cover his head and throat with his shaking hands. He wanted to protect himself from White Fang's teeth, of course. And that was the right thing to do. White Fang was beside himself with anger. Weedon Scott told his dog to calm down and leave the stranger alone. White Fang did so at once. Then the stranger got up carefully. It was Beauty Smith. A chain and a club lay next to him. Weedon and Matt looked at him with disgust. Then Weedon Scott put his hand on White Fang's neck and began to stroke him softly. "Did he try to steal you?" said Weedon Scott to White Fang. "And you attacked him so wildly! Well, he made a mistake. You attacked him like seventeen dogs at a time, White Fang. White Fang snarled at Beauty Smith angrily.
