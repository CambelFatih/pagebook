Chapter 14

The Long Journey

Summer was in the air. White Fang felt the coming change somehow. He did not know why and how, yet he had this feeling of the coming change from the gods themselves. White Fang guarded his master near the house, but he never went inside the house. One day, Weedon Scott and Matt were sitting by the fireplace in the house. "Just listen to that!" said Matt. Weedon Scott listened. Through the door came a loud howl. Then came a low noise near the door. White Fang heard his master's voice and understood that he was still inside. "I think that wolf feels your departure coming soon," said Matt. "What can I do with a wolf in California?" said Scott. "White dogs cannot fight against him at all. He can kill them easily. The authorities could take him away from me and kill him. No, I won't take him with me to California. It's impossible!" "But he loves you so much," said Matt. There was a short silence in the room. Weedon Scott thought to himself for some time. "I agree with you," said Scott. "He really loves me so much, and that's a big problem. But how can he feel my departure coming? How?" "I have no idea," answered Matt.
