Weedon Scott pushed her away and told her to go back to her place. The female dog obeyed him at once. The next moment many strange gods came out of the house. Some of them stood at a distance, but two of them went up to his master and hugged him kindly. White Fang watched them attentively. These gods also tried to come up to White Fang, but he snarled at them angrily. Collie watched White Fang carefully from a distance. She did not want to see a wolf in her house. She was sure the gods were making a mistake. Soon the gods entered the stone house. White Fang followed his master as usual. He was ready for all the unknown things inside of the house. But nothing dangerous happened in the house. He lay down at his master's feet and watched the gods the whole time.
