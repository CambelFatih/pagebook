Because he was the judge who had sent him to prison for fifty years. In the courtroom, Jim Hall had promised to kill him mercilessly. He said it was a matter of time. Then Jim Hall went to prison... and now that he had run away suddenly, the Judge knew that he would come for him. White Fang did not know about all this. But there was a secret between him and the master's wife. Each night, Alice went downstairs and let White Fang in to sleep in the big hall. Then each morning, she let him out before the family was awake. One night, all the house slept soundly. Suddenly, White Fang awoke and looked around silently. He sensed a strange god's smell in the house. Then he heard the sounds of the strange god's steps. White Fang did not bark, it was not his way. White Fang walked very softly. The strange god stopped at the stairs and listened, and White Fang watched him silently from a corner. White Fang waited. Then the strange god began to go upstairs carefully. At that moment White Fang attacked him without warning. He jumped on the strange god's back and began to bite him wildly. Together they fell to the floor. White Fang continued to bite the strange god without mercy. Sierra Vista awoke at once. There were many gun shots and cries. A man's voice cried in horror. Then there was an angry snarl. Suddenly, the noise stopped. The battle lasted only three minutes. The scared family ran to the bottom of the stairs quickly. Weedon Scott turned on the light and looked downstairs attentively.
