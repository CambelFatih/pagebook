Soon the whole family went to the most famous veterinarian in Sierra Vista. The veterinarian worked on White Fang for two hours. Then he came out to the family and looked at them sadly. "One broken leg," began the veterinarian, "and three broken ribs. He has lost too much blood. There are many deep scratches on his body, too. I took out three bullets from his body as well. His chances are one in ten thousand. That's my answer." "But he must have a chance. Please, do everything you can, doctor," said Weedon Scott. The whole family took care of White Fang for weeks. By some miracle he began to heal. White Fang was recovering very slowly. His desire to live was too strong. He had come from the wild, where only the strong ones can survive. That is why every part of White Fang fought for his life. He lay still for many weeks. He slept for a long time. His mind always dreamed of the Northland. Then came the day when Weedon Scott took off the last bandage on White Fang's body. It was a happy day. All Sierra Vista gathered around White Fang. The master stroked his ears, and he growled softly as usual. The master's wife called him the 'Blessed Wolf.' White Fang tried to rise to his feet and fell down with weakness. His muscles were too weak and soft at that moment. But he did not give up. Later with a little help, he stood on his feet and looked at his master happily.
