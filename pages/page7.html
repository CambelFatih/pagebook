She examined it with the greatest care. One Eye stood at the entrance and watched her silently. She lay down and put her head toward the entrance. She looked quite glad and . One Eye was hungry. He listened to the bright world around him joyfully. The weather was warm. The sun had come back. The awakening Northland was calling him. It was spring. Life was moving and growing under the snow. He looked at the she- wolf, but she did not want to get up. One Eye could not resist the call of the world anymore. besides, he was very hungry. So he went hunting alone. He was gone for about eight hours. At last, he came back and stopped in front of the cave in fear. He heard strange sounds from the cave. He tried to enter the cave, but the she-wolf snarled at him angrily. One Eye kept his distance. nevertheless, he saw between her legs five little cubs. They were very weak and helpless. Their eyes still did not open to the light of the sun. He was very surprised. The she-wolf watched him anxiously the whole time. Sometimes she snarled at him without a reason. At that moment, One Eye felt an old instinct from the generations of wolf fathers before him. He did not question it, nor puzzle over it. It was inside his being. He obeyed this instinct right away. He went hunting to find meat for his new-born family. Quickly, he came back with his prey in his mouth. The she-wolf looked at him and licked him on the neck kindly. She did not snarl at him anymore. He behaved like a good wolf-father. He showed his desire to take care of his family.
