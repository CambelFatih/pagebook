"No dog could have done what he did," said Judge Scott. "He is a Blessed Wolf," said Weedon Scott. "He'll learn to walk again. He can start right now. Let's take him outside." White Fang went outside slowly like a king. All of Sierra Vista watched him joyfully. White Fang's muscles were strong and he felt powerful again. Soon he reached the stables and lay down to rest for some time. There lay Collie, and ten puppies playing about her in the sun. White Fang looked at them with surprise. Then the master pushed one puppy toward him carefully. The puppy jumped around White Fang quickly. Then he touched White Fang's nose and licked him with his warm tongue. White Fang licked the puppy's face in return. There were many happy cries from the gods. Then Collie and the other puppies went up to him and lay next to him gladly. White Fang was very happy. He turned his head and watched them happily. The End
